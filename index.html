Programming journey

My interest for Tech didn't actually originate from software, about 3 years ago I got introduced to hardware or more specifically iphone hardware. I loved figuring out what very part did and how it worked. Later that same years I also looked into the world of custom PC's and what components are needed for a computer to run. Then, Myrthe or for most of you known as FoodieFarmer introduced me to programming, I love the idea of creating your own pieces of software to make your life easier. also as Josh said in boba talk #4, it gives the same feeling as building with Lego's. There was just one problem... although I loved the idea of programming, actually learning it was the biggest challenge. I tried 3 different courses, but every time I lost motivation be for I could learn the basics which let to me giving up. I stopped trying to learn any programming language for about half a year, until Foodie found Codédex and brought it to my attention. I love the way Codédex teaches, instantly applying everything you learn in to small and achievable projects is just the way to keep me motivated. Codédex worked for me and now I have a beginner understanding of not only Python but also HTML.
